Here you can personalize the certificates that will be displayed in the students section where you can print the selected certificates. We have three types of certificate category i.e. TC, CC, and SLC. Certificate customization has these

Stages:
  1. Check the box next to the name of the certificate you would like to see displayed in the students section.
  2. Click "Next/Skip" button according to your choices. You can skip any category if you found your required certificate is not listed in that list.
  3. Finally click "Save Setting" button to save your selection.
